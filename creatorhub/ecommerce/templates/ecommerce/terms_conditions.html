Terms of use

User Eligibility

This platform is built to serve the internal community of Jahangirnagar University. Only students who are currently enrolled at JU can register and sell products. Buyers are limited to JU students, teachers, university staff, or residents who live in close proximity to the university campus. Any attempt to use the platform from outside this intended community may result in restricted access or account suspension.

Account Registration

To maintain trust and transparency within the platform, all users must register using their real names and accurate personal information. Misrepresentation, including fake identities, using someone else‚Äôs name, or impersonating another person, is strictly forbidden. The platform may request ID verification if suspicious activity is detected, and false or misleading accounts will be removed without prior notice.

Product Listings

Sellers must provide truthful and detailed descriptions of their products, including clear photos, accurate pricing, and condition (new/used). Items that are illegal (such as narcotics, firearms), harmful (weapons, dangerous tools), or deceptive (counterfeit goods, plagiarized products) are strictly prohibited. Any violation will result in the immediate removal of listings and potential account suspension.

Orders & Payments

Buyers are responsible for reviewing product details, seller credibility, and agreed pricing before placing an order. All payments should be made through mutually accepted, secure methods such as mobile banking (e.g., bKash, Nagad), cash on delivery, or bank transfer. The platform does not process payments directly and cannot mediate in financial disputes between parties.

Delivery & Pickup

The platform does not have any logistics service of its own. Delivery arrangements, including location, time, and method (courier, hand delivery, etc.) must be decided between buyer and seller. Delays, lost packages, or other delivery-related issues are to be resolved between the two parties. The platform is not liable for transportation faults or miscommunication.

Refund & Return Policy

All return, exchange, or refund claims must be handled through mutual understanding between the buyer and seller. The platform does not guarantee any money-back policy or intervention in case of dissatisfaction. Sellers are encouraged to clearly mention their return/refund terms in their product descriptions. Buyers should clarify such policies before completing a purchase.

Prohibited Activities

Users are strictly prohibited from engaging in the following:
- Using the platform for activities unrelated to commerce (e.g., political propaganda, spam)
- Harassment, abusive language, or threats
- Fraudulent behavior such as fake orders, price manipulation
- Promoting or selling illegal or stolen goods
Violating these terms may result in temporary suspension or permanent banning of the account, and in some cases, legal action.

Changes to Terms

The platform reserves the right to modify these Terms & Conditions at any time. Major updates will be posted on the website. It is the responsibility of the user to stay informed by reviewing the Terms periodically. Continued use of the platform after changes implies agreement to the updated policies.
