Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect basic personal information such as your name, student ID, contact number, and location to ensure that users are part of the Jahangirnagar University community. We also gather usage data including pages visited, time spent, and transactions made for analytical and service improvement purposes.

How Your Information is Used

Your data is used to verify your identity, manage your account, process transactions, and offer customer support. We may use aggregate (non-identifiable) data to improve our platform's functionality and user experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information may only be shared with service providers (e.g., for email delivery or analytics) under strict confidentiality agreements. We will only disclose information if legally required.

Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies to personalize your experience and track site usage. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly.

Data Security

We employ reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.
